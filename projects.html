Ducks of a Feather

I work with an awesome group of people that connects name image and likeness (NIL) rights with student athletes at The University of Oregon! In this project I helped setup and maintain a Discord server along with a custom bot for fun engagement with the community. Similar to the Duck bot, this was build with discord.py and uses a few commands to allow for access to animated emojis, and auto-reacts to keywords in chat. Here's the Ducks of a Feather website.
